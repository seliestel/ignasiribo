CALL FOR PAPERS

5th ASLE/ASEAN Ecocritical Conference

23-25 November 2023

We invite scholars, students, artists, activists, and anyone interested in the environmental humanities to submit proposals for paper presentations (lasting no more than 20 minutes) on relevant topics, including (but not limited to):

Posthumanist theory and criticism Monsters, cyborgs, and other hybrids
Anthropocene, Chthulucene, Capitalocene, etc. Eco/bioart, environmental film and drama, and other ecomedia
Critical animal and plant studies Posthuman and postnatural geographies
Multispecies environments and ethnologies Eco-cosmopolitanism and bioregionalism
Nonhuman and hybrid agencies Non-anthropocentric and unnatural writing
Indigenous and traditional ecological knowledge Critical posthumanism
Posthuman ecopoetics Posthuman pedagogies
Ecosemiotics, biosemiotics, and interspecies communication New materialisms and material ecocriticism
Environmental philosophy and ethics beyond the human Toxic embodiment and environmental health
Environmental justice across species and other boundaries Disability and posthumanism
Postcolonial ecocriticism Dystopia, utopia, and speculative futures
Ecofeminism and queer posthumanisms Transhumanisms and human/machine interaction

The conference will be held onsite at Mae Fah Luang University, in the province of Chiang Rai, Thailand. However, this format might have to be altered depending on the evolution of the COVID-19 pandemic. Any changes will be duly announced on this webpage and through the ASLE/ASEAN website. The official language of the conference is English, but we will accept oral presentations in any ASEAN language as long as you provide us with a written translation of your paper into English ahead of the conference.

If you are interested in presenting a paper at the conference, please submit your abstract (max. 250 words) and a short bio note (max. 100 words) using the following link only (do not send it by email):

Submit abstract
  • Submit your abstract proposal before 31 August 2023.
  • We will notify if your proposal is accepted/rejected within 8 weeks of submission.
  • If accepted, register for the conference between 15 January and 15 October 2023 (you will be informed how to register after your abstract has been accepted).
  • Registration fees paid for the conference will also provide a 1-year membership of ASLE/ASEAN.
  • The conference programme will be announced on 1 November 2023.
  • Papers presented at the conference will be considered for publication in a special issue of the Journal of Southeast Asian Ecocriticism.
  • Conference dates: Thursday 23, Friday 24, and Saturday 25 November 2023.
  • Conference location: Mae Fah Luang University, Chiang Rai, Thailand.
  • For more details, please follow the updates published on this website.
  • If you have any queries, you can contact Dr Ignasi Ribó, School of Liberal Arts, Mae Fah Luang University, via email (asleasean@mfu.ac.th).
